Provide the audience with an outdoor activity to help build teamwork, professional communication, an opportunity to explore the outdoors, memorable adventures and physical growth

Audience

1. Who are the target customers? Those who search for outdoor activities to participate in.

2. What do they want? The target audience wants a fun outdoor activity with memorable adventures and fun.

3. What do they expect? Their expectations is that everything should be exactly as what they want. It could also involve what some other fun activities. It is also our duty to make these possible.
